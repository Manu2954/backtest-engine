# Version 1 - Current Limitations and Constraints

Generated: 2026-03-08
Status: All 10 critical/high/medium bugs fixed and merged

---

## 1. POSITION MANAGEMENT

### Single Position Only
- Only supports ONE position at a time (all-in or all-out)
- Cannot scale in/out of positions (e.g., 50% entry, then add 25% more later)
- Cannot hold multiple positions across different assets simultaneously
- No portfolio-level backtesting (single ticker only per backtest)
- Cannot pyramid into winning positions
- Cannot dollar-cost average into a single position over multiple signals

Impact: Limits realistic trading simulation where traders often scale positions


---

## 2. ORDER TYPES

### Market Orders Only
- Only supports market orders executed at next bar's open price
- No support for:
  * Limit orders (buy/sell at specific price)
  * Stop orders (trigger at specific price level)
  * Market-on-close orders
  * Trailing stop orders as entry/exit signals (only as exit mechanism)
  * Good-til-canceled (GTC) orders
  * Fill-or-kill (FOK) orders

Impact: Cannot model realistic order execution strategies


---

## 3. POSITION SIZING

### Limited Sizing Methods
Current methods:
  - full_capital: Use all available cash
  - percent_capital: Use X% of available cash
  - fixed_amount: Use fixed dollar amount

Missing methods:
  - Kelly Criterion (optimal bet sizing)
  - Risk-based sizing (% of equity risked per trade based on stop distance)
  - Volatility-adjusted sizing (ATR-based)
  - Maximum shares/dollar constraints combined with other methods
  - Fixed fractional (risk fixed % of total equity, not just cash)
  - Optimal f (Ralph Vince)

Impact: Cannot implement professional risk management strategies


---

## 4. DATA SOURCES

### Yahoo Finance Only
- Single data provider: yfinance library
- Subject to Yahoo Finance API limitations:
  * Rate limiting
  * Data quality issues
  * Historical data gaps
  * Service outages
  * Intraday data limited to last 60 days for stocks

Missing capabilities:
  - Multiple data providers (Alpha Vantage, IEX Cloud, Polygon.io, etc.)
  - Custom CSV data import
  - Real-time or tick data
  - Alternative data (fundamentals, sentiment, news, etc.)
  - Cryptocurrency exchanges (Binance, Coinbase Pro)
  - Futures and options data

Impact: Limited data quality and coverage, single point of failure


---

## 5. TIME RESOLUTION

### Fixed Resolution Constraints
- Stocks: Intraday history limited to 60 days (Yahoo Finance constraint)
- No sub-minute resolutions (lowest is 1m)
- Fixed resolution per backtest (can't mix daily strategy with intraday data)
- No tick-by-tick data support

Impact: Cannot test high-frequency strategies or combine timeframes


---

## 6. CONDITION ENGINE

### Single-Level Logic Only
- Only supports single-level AND/OR within a condition group
- Cannot create nested logic: (A AND B) OR (C AND D)
- Current structure:
  * Entry Group: Condition1 AND/OR Condition2 AND/OR Condition3
  * Exit Group: Condition1 AND/OR Condition2 AND/OR Condition3

Missing capabilities:
  - Arithmetic operations in conditions (e.g., "SMA_20 - SMA_50 > 10")
  - Time-based conditions (e.g., "only trade between 9:30-10:30 AM")
  - Day-of-week filters (e.g., "no trades on Mondays")
  - Inter-bar conditions (e.g., "highest high in last 20 bars")
  - Lookback comparisons (e.g., "close > close[5]" - 5 bars ago)
  - Pattern recognition (e.g., "bullish engulfing candle")
  - Volume conditions (no direct volume operators)

Supported operators only:
  - GT, LT, EQ, GTE, LTE (basic comparison)
  - CROSSES_ABOVE, CROSSES_BELOW (crossover detection)
  - IS_RISING, IS_FALLING (trend detection)

Impact: Cannot implement complex trading rules or filters


---

## 7. INDICATORS

### Pre-defined Indicators Only
Supported indicators (via pandas-ta):
  - SMA, EMA (moving averages)
  - RSI (momentum oscillator)
  - MACD (trend/momentum)
  - Bollinger Bands (volatility)
  - ATR (volatility)
  - Stochastic (momentum)
  - ADX (trend strength)
  - Ichimoku Cloud (trend/support/resistance)

Missing capabilities:
  - Custom indicator formulas (user-defined calculations)
  - Indicator-on-indicator (e.g., "RSI of momentum", "SMA of ATR")
  - Multi-timeframe indicators (e.g., daily SMA on 1h chart)
  - Composite indicators combining price and volume
  - Custom aggregations (e.g., "sum of close over 10 bars")
  - Percentage change calculations
  - Highest/lowest over N periods (except via Ichimoku)

Impact: Limited to common indicators, cannot implement proprietary systems


---

## 8. EXIT STRATEGIES

### Limited Exit Options
Current exit methods:
  - Exit signal from condition engine
  - Percentage-based stop loss (static)
  - Percentage-based take profit (static)
  - Dynamic stop (single indicator reference, e.g., ATR trailing)
  - Force close at end of backtest

Missing capabilities:
  - Multiple trailing stops simultaneously (e.g., both ATR AND percentage)
  - Time-based exits (e.g., "exit after 10 days" or "exit at 3:55 PM")
  - Profit target laddering (e.g., take 50% at 2R, 50% at 4R)
  - Chandelier stops (ATR-based from highest high)
  - Parabolic SAR exits
  - Break-even stops (move stop to entry after profit)
  - Reward-to-risk ratio targets (e.g., exit at 3:1 R:R)
  - Volatility-adjusted exits

Impact: Cannot implement sophisticated exit strategies


---

## 9. TRADING DIRECTION

### Long Only
- Only supports LONG positions (buy low, sell high)
- No short selling (sell high, buy low)
- No inverse strategies

Impact: Cannot test bear market strategies or hedging


---

## 10. PERFORMANCE & SCALABILITY

### Single-Threaded Execution
- Backtests run one at a time (one ticker per backtest)
- No parallel execution across multiple tickers
- Celery worker processes tasks sequentially

Missing capabilities:
  - Batch backtesting (test strategy across 100 tickers at once)
  - Parameter optimization/scanning (test 1000 parameter combinations)
  - Walk-forward analysis (rolling window optimization)
  - Monte Carlo simulation (randomize entry/exit timing)
  - Genetic algorithm optimization
  - Multi-core/distributed backtesting

Cache limitations:
  - Redis cache has no clustering support
  - Single Redis instance (single point of failure)
  - No cache invalidation strategy beyond TTL

Impact: Slow for portfolio-wide analysis or optimization


---

## 11. RISK MANAGEMENT

### Basic Risk Controls Only
Current features:
  - Commission (flat + percentage)
  - Slippage (percentage-based)
  - Stop loss (percentage)
  - Take profit (percentage)
  - Position sizing (basic methods)

Missing capabilities:
  - Maximum drawdown stop (halt strategy when DD exceeds threshold)
  - Daily loss limits (stop trading after losing X% in one day)
  - Correlation tracking across backtests
  - Position concentration limits (max % of portfolio per position)
  - Leverage/margin simulation
  - Margin calls
  - Advanced commission models (tiered, maker/taker)
  - Borrowing costs (for shorts)
  - Overnight holding fees
  - Currency conversion fees (for international assets)

Impact: Cannot model realistic broker constraints and risk limits


---

## 12. REPORTING & ANALYTICS

### Basic Metrics Only
Current metrics:
  - Total return, win rate, profit factor
  - Sharpe ratio, max drawdown
  - Average win/loss, largest win/loss
  - Trade count, trade duration
  - Buy-and-hold comparison

Missing analytics:
  - Sortino ratio (downside deviation)
  - Calmar ratio (return/max drawdown)
  - Omega ratio
  - Value at Risk (VaR)
  - Conditional VaR (CVaR)
  - Monte Carlo analysis
  - Equity curve comparison across strategies
  - Trade distribution histograms
  - Win/loss streaks analysis
  - Seasonal/time-of-day analysis
  - Slippage impact analysis (beyond fixed percentage)
  - Rolling returns analysis
  - Underwater equity curve (drawdown visualization)
  - MAE/MFE analysis (maximum adverse/favorable excursion)

Impact: Limited performance analysis and risk assessment


---

## 13. USER MANAGEMENT

### No Authentication System
Current state:
  - Basic User model exists in database
  - No login/logout functionality
  - No password hashing
  - No session management
  - No API authentication

Missing capabilities:
  - User registration/authentication
  - Role-based access control (admin, user, read-only)
  - Multi-tenancy (isolated user data)
  - API keys for programmatic access
  - Rate limiting per user
  - User quotas (max strategies, max backtests)
  - Resource limits (CPU time, memory)
  - Audit logging (who did what, when)

Impact: Not production-ready, no security


---

## 14. REAL-TIME FEATURES

### Historical Backtesting Only
- No live trading integration
- No paper trading mode (simulated live trading)
- No real-time alerts when backtest completes
- No WebSocket support for streaming results
- No live data feeds

Impact: Cannot transition from backtest to live trading


---

## 15. DATA QUALITY

### Basic Validation Only
Current handling:
  - Gap detection (heuristic-based, checks bar count vs expected)
  - Yahoo Finance auto-adjust for splits/dividends
  - Basic OHLCV validation (columns exist)

Missing capabilities:
  - Outlier detection (e.g., price spikes from bad data)
  - Bad tick filtering
  - Missing data interpolation options (forward fill, linear, etc.)
  - Data alignment across multiple tickers
  - Corporate action handling (splits, dividends, mergers)
  - Survivorship bias handling (delisted stocks)
  - Point-in-time data (avoid look-ahead bias)

Impact: May backtest on incorrect or incomplete data


---

## 16. STRATEGY VERSIONING

### No Version Control
- Editing a strategy modifies it permanently
- All historical backtests reference the same strategy object
- No way to revert to previous strategy version
- No diff/comparison between strategy versions

Missing capabilities:
  - Strategy version history
  - Rollback to previous version
  - Compare strategy versions side-by-side
  - Tag/label strategy versions (e.g., "v1.0-production")
  - A/B testing framework (test two strategy variants)
  - Strategy cloning/templating

Impact: Cannot track strategy evolution or reproduce historical results


---

## 17. COSTS & FEES

### Simple Commission Model
Current model:
  - Flat commission per trade (e.g., $5)
  - Percentage commission (e.g., 0.1%)
  - Applied to both entry and exit

Missing cost types:
  - Borrowing costs (for short positions)
  - Overnight holding fees (for leveraged positions)
  - Currency conversion fees (for international assets)
  - Tax simulation (short-term vs long-term capital gains)
  - Spread costs (bid-ask spread)
  - Market impact (large orders moving the market)
  - Maker/taker fee models (crypto exchanges)
  - Tiered commission schedules (lower fees for high volume)

Impact: Overly optimistic returns, especially for frequent trading


---

### Basic Interface Only
Current state (as per CLAUDE.md):
